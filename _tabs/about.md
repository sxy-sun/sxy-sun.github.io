I'm a robotics engineer with a M.S. in Robotics from the [University of Michigan Robotics Department](https://robotics.umich.edu/) (2023) and a B.S. in Computer Science from [Rutgers University](https://www.cs.rutgers.edu/) (2021).

I work in R&D, building full-stack robotics systems that span embedded firmware, real-time communication, and autonomous software. My day-to-day tools include ROS 2, microROS, RViz, rqt, and Foxglove, and I work with microcontrollers and Raspberry Pi systems.

As of 2026, we are experiencing a very difficult time, a downturn for most of us. But I believe good engineers remain versatile. There is not much we can do to change the timing when it is our turn, but we can always learn what needs to be learned and do what needs to be done.

“时也，运也，命也，非吾之所能也。”
