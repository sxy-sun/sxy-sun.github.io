I'm a robotics engineer with a M.S. in Robotics from the [University of Michigan Robotics Department](https://robotics.umich.edu/) (2023) and a B.S. in Computer Science from [Rutgers University](https://www.cs.rutgers.edu/) (2021).

I build full-stack robotics systems that span embedded firmware, real-time communication, and autonomous software. My day-to-day tools include ROS 2, microROS, RViz, rqt, and Foxglove, and I work with microcontrollers and Raspberry Pi systems as well.

After all, “时也，运也，命也，非吾之所能也。”
